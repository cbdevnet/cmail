Build prerequisites

cmail-msa
	libgnutls28-dev		When compiling without CMAIL_NO_TLS
	libnettle-dev		(Comes with libgnutls)
	libsqlite3-dev

cmail-popd
	libgnutls28-dev		When compiling without CMAIL_NO_TLS
	libnettle-dev		(Comes with libgnutls)
	libsqlite3-dev

Building is as simple as running make in the module directories on a
system with a working C compiler (tcc is fine).
